## Banking (EU Passport Workaround)

Open with EU residency, then change address to Paraguay. Cards and limits typically remain active.

| Service | Fees | Currencies | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Wise](https://wise.com) | ACH send $1.13; ACH receive free; EUR free | USD, EUR | $20M receive limit; cards stay active after residency change |
| [Paysera](https://www.paysera.com) | 30 free SEPA/mo then €0.30 | EUR | Order card before switching residency |
| [Genome](https://genome.eu/) | See site | EUR | Non-nominal; PY address shows on bank statements for KYC use |
| [SOLO](https://solo.ge/en) | See site | GEL, USD, EUR, GBP | Georgian bank; requires EU passport + PY residency + income proof |
| [HSBC HK](https://www.hsbc.com.hk) | HKD 10,000/mo minimum (~$1,279) | HKD + Asian currencies | Open in allowed country first; PY card delivery not supported |
